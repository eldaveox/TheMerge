HOW WE MERGE GENRES

A whole new world is opening up at Merge Festival, far from any restrictions. Be it our cosmopolitan crowd or diversity embedded in all aspects of the festival – in our unique setting we can celebrate without prejudices all weekend long. Between beautiful beaches and massive iron giants, all of the lifestyle and fashion scene’s music connoisseurs and trendsetters gather, merging into a limitless creative flow thanks to an authentic affinity for underground music culture capturing the zeitgeist. At the Merge, genre borders are broken down as well: vibes on all stages oscillate between hard techno sets, soulful sax samples, grunge-y guitar riffs, thumping 808s and punchlines.

WHAT TO EXPECT AT THE MERGE?

We started a unique new music festival in Vienna merging genres and trends of avant-garde jazz, hip hop, techno, IDM, folk, electronica, folktronica, and more. What can you do at the festival? All things avant-garde and danceable: Listen to a huge array of music, dance in the open air, experiment with sound, witness a high-tech light show, or experience trance parties. With lightning-fast line-up changes and secret masterpieces you never get to hear anywhere else, this is the only festival that gives you the possibility to discover new and exciting music every night. 50+ hours of music on the same stage. 6 stages, 5 rooms, 4 platforms. Unexplored festival. A festival for music lovers. A festival for thinkers. A festival for anyone with a little ear... Beside the open air mainstage, 4 terraces will offer the chance to listen to music, dance, or experiment with sound: 1 room on the ground, 1 room on the second level, 2 platforms (on the ground and the 2nd level). Listen to an avant-garde and danceable music style like Electro House or Trip Hop. Get to know the emerging sounds of underground electronic music. There's something for everyone here: soundsystems, DJs, musicians and dancers. Each stage offers a different kind of music and an offer a new experience every day! Can you put some more numbers on it? 200 performers in 50+ hours of music 10 000 people from more than 50 countries Each day we will play experimental music in 20 different sound-systems (including speakers mounted on an Ubersuite, a solar powered mobile speaker tower, even a megaphone)

WHERE TO FIND US

Our festival is located right next to the danube river. this gives a special charm to the whole festival as people can walk on the water and watch the festival live from the shore of the river. you can come up in speed boats to the festival where you can also do a couple of waterside practices.
